Two world wars brought devastation and heroism in equal measure. The Cunard Yanks brought American culture into the city's bloodstream, feeding directly into the Merseybeat revolution that briefly made Liverpool the centre of the world's attention. And then, with brutal speed, containerisation made the traditional dock economy obsolete, and the city that had seemed indestructible began to unravel."
